diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body either doesn’t produce enough insulin or can't effectively use it, leading to high blood sugar levels. It requires careful management to prevent complications like heart disease, nerve damage, and vision loss.

covid-19 vaccine

COVID-19 vaccines are designed to train the immune system to recognize and fight the SARS-CoV-2 virus, helping to prevent severe illness, hospitalization, and death.
